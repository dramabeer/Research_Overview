Research Summary

Anna Beer is a post-doctoral researcher as part of the Data Mining and Machine Learning group, at University of Vienna's faculty of Computer Science. Her focus lies on foundational research in unsupervised machine learning data mining, and the evaluation of methods in this field. On this page, you can find an overview of research projects, supervised bachelor and master theses, as well as tutorials and other posts on topics within this realm.
