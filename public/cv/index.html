About Me

I am a post-doctoral researcher in Data Mining and Machine Learning at the University of Vienna. I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from LMU Munich for my work “On the Edges of Clustering - Creating Synergies with Related Problems”, supervised by Prof. Dr. Thomas Seidl.
Before my current position, I was working on data analysis methods for molecular dynamics data as a post-doc at Aarhus University, Denmark.

Research Interests:
Unsupervised Machine Learning
Density-based Clustering
Evaluation of AI Methods
